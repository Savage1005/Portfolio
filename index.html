About Me

Hello! I am Tom Savage, a full stack web development student. I am originally from Williamsburg, Virginia, and I currently live in Pineville, North Carolina. I have a degree in creative writing from Appalachian State. Before becoming a student, I was in the beer business where I worked my way up from an entry level position as a tap line cleaner to becoming a professional brewer that created a beer that won the gold medal in the US Open!

The beer business is a physical one, however, and is a job of passion, so it is not the best paying occupation. Now, I have decided move in a different direction and pursue a new career in a field that I also have an immense passion for, computers! I am looking forward to the new challenges ahead! Cheers!
